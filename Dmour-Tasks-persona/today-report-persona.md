## What Was Done

### 1. Image Prompt Development

- **Created prompt templates** for generating persona card images (avatar + background style + priority color coding)
- **Generated images** for all 10 personas using AI tools, following RTL-friendly design guidelines
- **Established visual standards:**
  - Stylized 3D, realistic adult proportions (non-chibi)
  - Pure white background (no environments, no clutter)
  - Soft even studio lighting with subtle rim light
  - Warm desaturated palette

### 2. Video Prompt Development

- **Concept:** Each persona enters a modern lobby space, with 3–6 seconds of visible gesture reflecting their identity
- **Example (Abu Mohammad):** Enters with calm dignity, hands clasping envelope at chest (pensioner signature gesture)
- **Camera:** Smooth steady follow from behind at waist height

### 3. Persona Psychology Deep Dive

Extensive discussion covered the behavioral and emotional architecture of each archetype:
- Core tensions (military vs. irrelevance, meritocracy vs. wasta)
- Emotional drivers (fear of shame, stagnation, statelessness)
- Gesture language (the envelope tap as "pension, paperwork, bureaucracy")

### 4. Prepared Persona Cards with:
- Dashboard tagline (short, e.g., "Disillusioned Pensioner")
- Priority badge (P1/P2/P3)
- Behavioral tags
- Representative Arabic phrase

---

## Key Lessons Learned

1. **Visual tone must match emotional weight.** A persona defined by "stagnation and humiliation" cannot be rendered as cute or toy-like (Tareq chibi experiment → rejected).

2. **White backgrounds are a unifying convention.** They force focus onto the character and prevent image generators from adding distracting environmental narratives.

3. **Specificity beats generality.** "Deep warm brown skin" generates better results than "dark skin."

4. **Gesture is narrative.** The right gesture in 4 seconds can say "pension, paperwork, bureaucracy" without a single word.
