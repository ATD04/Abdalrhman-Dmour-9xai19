  Use Cases for the Strategic Plan (0206)
   * Policy & Investment Analysis: Ask the LLM to identify priority projects for the next three years to help in budget
     planning or attracting international grants.
   * Risk Assessment: Have the model summarize the ministry's strategy for dealing with climate change and population
     growth to generate reports for stakeholders.
   * Internal Alignment: Use the LLM to check if a new proposed local project aligns with the national strategic pillars
     defined in this document.


  Use Cases for the Services Guide (0002)
   * Smart Citizen Chatbot: Build a RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) system where a citizen asks: "What are the
     requirements for a new water meter in Amman?" and the LLM provides a precise, verified answer.
   * Staff Training Assistant: New employees can use the LLM to quickly learn complex administrative workflows without
     needing to read hundreds of pages manually.
   * Automated Document Verification: The LLM can be used to generate a "Checklist" for any specific service, which can
     then be used to automatically verify if a citizen's application is complete.
