1. 0002_الخطة_الاستراتيجية_2023-2025.pdf (Strategic Plan 2023-2025)
  This is the foundational document that defines the Ministry's identity and roadmap.


   * Why it's the best: It contains the high-level vision, mission, strategic goals, and Key Performance Indicators
     (KPIs). It explains the "Why" behind every regulation and project.
   * Use Cases:
       * Policy Inquiries: Answering questions about the Ministry's long-term goals for the industrial and trade
         sectors.
       * Strategic Alignment: Helping businesses or researchers align their projects with national priorities.
       * Goal Tracking: Identifying which sectors (e.g., SMEs, exports, consumer protection) are currently prioritized.


  2. 0066_التقرير_السنوي_2024-0.pdf (Annual Report 2024)
  This is the most recent "performance" document, providing concrete data and summaries of activities.


   * Why it's the best: While the Strategic Plan is about the future, the Annual Report is about the recent past and
     present. It contains statistics on registered companies, patents granted, trade volumes, and specific challenges
     faced in the last year.
   * Use Cases:
       * Data Extraction: Answering questions like "How many trademarks were registered in 2024?" or "What were the
         major trade achievements last year?"
       * Performance Auditing: Summarizing the Ministry's actual progress against its stated goals.
       * Trend Analysis: Helping users understand current market dynamics based on the Ministry’s recorded data.
