

  A. Gap Analysis & Decision Support
   * Prompt Example: "Based on the goals in the ESP and the results in the 2023 M&E report, which strategic pillars are
     furthest behind their targets, and what are the cited reasons for the delay?"
   * Value: Automatically identifies underperforming areas to help officials prioritize resources.


  B. Stakeholder & Donor Reporting
   * Prompt Example: "Draft a 3-page progress brief for an international donor summarizing our achievements in 'Quality
     of Education' during 2023, using the specific metrics found in the M&E report."
   * Value: Reduces the time spent by Ministry staff on manual report writing from days to minutes.


  C. Policy Alignment Checker
   * Prompt Example: "I am drafting a new initiative for teacher training. Does this align with the priorities set out
     in the Education Strategic Plan? Point out any contradictions."
   * Value: Ensures that new projects or local initiatives remain consistent with the national high-level strategy.


  D. Institutional "Brain" (Q&A)
   * Prompt Example: "What is the Ministry's specific target for Kindergarten (KG2) enrollment by the end of the plan,
     and what was the actual enrollment rate recorded in 2023?"
   * Value: Provides instant, cited answers to technical questions that would otherwise require searching through
     hundreds of pages.
