
  1. Document: 0003_دليل_الخدمات_2023__1_.pdf (Service Directory 2023)
  This is the most critical document for any citizen-facing or operational AI application. It likely contains a detailed
  catalog of all services provided by the Ministry, including requirements, procedures, and locations.


   * Why it's one of the best: It serves as the "source of truth" for what the Ministry actually does on a day-to-day
     basis. It is data-rich and highly structured.
   * Use Cases:
       * Citizen Support Chatbot: An AI that can answer questions like "How do I renew my health insurance?" or "What
         documents are needed for a pharmaceutical license?"
       * Automated Service Routing: Helping users identify which department or facility they need to visit based on
         their specific health or administrative needs.
       * Internal Knowledge Base: A tool for Ministry employees to quickly find specific service protocols without
         manually searching through large PDF directories.


  2. Document: 0079_الخطة_الإستراتيجية_لوزارة_الصحة_للأعوام_2023___2025.pdf (Strategic Plan 2023-2025)
  This document provides the vision, mission, strategic goals, and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) of the Ministry.


   * Why it's one of the best: While the Service Directory covers the "How," this document covers the "Why" and "What's
     Next." It provides the LLM with the necessary context to understand the Ministry’s priorities (e.g., digital
     transformation, primary care expansion).
   * Use Cases:
       * Policy Analysis & Planning: Assisting health officials in drafting new initiatives that align with the
         established 2023–2025 goals.
       * Performance Monitoring AI: An LLM can use this to compare current performance reports (like the 2023 Annual
         Report) against the strategic targets to identify gaps.
       * Stakeholder & Donor Reporting: Automatically generating summaries or presentations for international partners
         (like the World Bank) showing how specific projects contribute to the national health strategy.
